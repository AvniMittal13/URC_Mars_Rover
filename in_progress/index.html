Exploring Mars - The Red Planet

Team Deimos is a group of young, enthusiastic students from IIT Mandi who are designing and building high-tech Mars rover.

Who Are We

As students IIT Mandi, we aspire to make our thoughts a reality by producing and hard at work on our projects. Our prototypes compete in a number of international and national competitions. The projects are intended to be a teaching and learning experience for those interested in mechanical engineering, software, electronics, or robotics and exposes us to a variety of other engineering fields, both practical and theoretical, broadening our expertise. As a group, we learn to grasp industry work cultures and problem-solving in real-time, all while adhering to a planned deadline.
